Frequently Asked Questions

Is Preppr free to use?

Yes! Preppr offers a free plan with basic features. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited meal planning, advanced features, and more recipes.

Can I add my own recipes?

Absolutely! You can add unlimited custom recipes with photos, ingredients, instructions, and nutrition info.

Does it work offline?

Yes! Once you've downloaded your meal plan and recipes, you can access them offline while shopping or cooking.

Can I share meal plans with family?

Yes! Pro users can share meal plans and grocery lists with family members for collaborative planning.

What platforms does Preppr support?

Preppr is available on iOS, Android, and web. Your data syncs seamlessly across all devices.
